<p><p>Medical non-absorbable sutures are surgical threads used to stitch tissues that require permanent support and stability during the healing process. Unlike absorbable sutures, which dissolve over time, non-absorbable sutures remain intact until they are physically removed or are naturally integrated into the body. They are widely used in various surgical procedures, including orthopedics, cardiovascular surgeries, and wound closures, owing to their superior tensile strength and durability.</p><p>The Medical Non-Absorbable Suture Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period.
