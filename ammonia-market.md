<p><p>The ammonia market comprises two primary types: liquid ammonia and gas ammonia. Liquid ammonia is typically used for agricultural fertilizers and industrial applications, stored under pressure for ease of transport and handling. In contrast, gas ammonia exists as a vapor at room temperature, primarily utilized in refrigeration and as an intermediate in chemical processes. Both forms play crucial roles in various industries, with liquid ammonia being more popular in agricultural applications, while gas ammonia serves broader industrial and chemical needs.</p></p>
