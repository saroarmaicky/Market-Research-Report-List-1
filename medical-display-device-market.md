<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>OLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical display device market consists of two primary types: LED and OLED displays. LED displays use light-emitting diodes to produce images, offering high brightness and energy efficiency, making them suitable for diagnostic and surgical imaging. In contrast, OLED displays utilize organic compounds that emit light when an electric current passes through, providing superior contrast, faster response times, and wider viewing angles. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing image quality and improving the accuracy of medical diagnoses.</p></p>
