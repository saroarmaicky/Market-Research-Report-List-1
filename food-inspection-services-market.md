<p><ul><li>During Production Inspection</li><li>Final Random Inspection</li><li>Loading Supervision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food inspection services encompass three key market types. During Production Inspection involves monitoring food safety and quality standards while the product is being manufactured, ensuring compliance throughout the process. Final Random Inspection occurs once production is complete, assessing a batch to certify its adherence to regulations before distribution. Loading Supervision focuses on overseeing the transfer of food products to shipping containers, ensuring that hygiene and safety measures are maintained during transportation, thus safeguarding food quality until delivery.</p></p>
