<p><p>The Arabinogalactan market includes two primary types: Adhesive-bonded Cross-Laminated Timber (CLT) and Mechanically Fastened CLT. Adhesive-bonded CLT utilizes adhesives to bond layers of wood, providing a strong, durable structure suitable for various applications. In contrast, Mechanically Fastened CLT relies on physical fasteners like screws and bolts for assembly, offering versatility and ease of construction. Both types leverage Arabinogalactan, a natural polysaccharide that enhances adhesion and mechanical properties, contributing to the sustainability and performance of CLT products.</p></p>
