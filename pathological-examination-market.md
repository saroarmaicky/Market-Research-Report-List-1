<p><p>The Pathological Examination Market encompasses various segments, including Histopathology, Cytopathology, Immunohistochemical Pathology, and Molecular Pathology. Histopathology involves the examination of tissue samples to diagnose diseases, while Cytopathology focuses on individual cell analysis for cancer detection and other conditions. Immunohistochemical Pathology employs antibodies to identify specific proteins in tissues, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning. Lastly, Molecular Pathology utilizes molecular biology techniques to study genetic mutations and biomarkers, enhancing personalized medicine and targeted therapies. Together, these segments enhance diagnostic accuracy and patient care.</p></p>
