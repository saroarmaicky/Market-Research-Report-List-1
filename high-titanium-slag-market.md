<p><p>The high titanium slag market includes two main types: acid-soluble slag and chlorination slag. Acid-soluble slag is characterized by its ability to dissolve in acids, allowing for further processing to extract titanium dioxide and other valuable minerals. In contrast, chlorination slag involves using chlorine in the extraction process, which can enhance the purity and yield of titanium products. Both types cater to different industrial applications, influencing production methods and end-user requirements in the titanium supply chain.</p></p>
