<p><p>The Laboratory Gas Pipeline System market features two primary types: Semi-Automatic Switchover Systems and Fully Automatic Switchover Systems. Semi-Automatic systems allow manual intervention for gas source switching, offering flexibility and control, which is ideal for varied applications. Conversely, Fully Automatic systems enable seamless and efficient gas source transitions without human input, enhancing safety and reliability by minimizing operator error. Both systems cater to laboratories' needs for continuous gas supply while ensuring optimal performance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
