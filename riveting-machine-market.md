<p><p>The riveting machine market encompasses various technologies: Impact riveting uses a hammering action to join materials, while orbital riveting subjects the rivet to rotating pressure for consistent fastening. Radial (spiralform) riveting forms rivets in a circular motion, providing a secure bond. Rollerform riveting employs rollers to deform rivets, ensuring tight connections. Automatic drilling and riveting machines combine drilling and riveting functions, enhancing efficiency and precision in manufacturing processes. Together, these methods cater to diverse industrial applications and production needs.</p></p>
