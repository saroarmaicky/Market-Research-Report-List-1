<p><strong>The Amorphous Core Distribution Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-immersed</li><li>Dry-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Amorphous Core Distribution Transformer market includes two main types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers utilize insulating oil to cool and insulate the core and windings, allowing for efficient heat dissipation and higher power ratings. In contrast, dry-type transformers use air as the cooling medium, making them safer and more environmentally friendly. While oil-immersed transformers are preferred for high-power applications, dry-type transformers are often used in urban areas where safety and reduced fire risk are paramount.</p></p>
