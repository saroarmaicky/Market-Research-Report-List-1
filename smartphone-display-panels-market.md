<p><p>The smartphone display panels market primarily features four types: LCD, IPS-LCD, OLED, and AMOLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) is widely used for its affordability and brightness. IPS-LCD (In-Plane Switching LCD) offers better color accuracy and viewing angles. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) provides deeper blacks and improved contrast, as each pixel emits its own light. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) further enhances this technology with faster refresh rates and more vibrant colors, making it the preferred choice for high-end devices.</p></p>
