<p><p>The lawn sweeper market consists primarily of two types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are manually operated and ideal for smaller lawns, offering ease of use and maneuverability. They typically feature a lightweight design, making them suitable for quick cleanups. In contrast, tow-behind sweepers are designed for larger properties and attach to riding mowers or tractors. These units cover more ground efficiently, making them practical for extensive areas, saving time and effort for the user.</p></p>
