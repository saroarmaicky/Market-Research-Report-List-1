<p><p>PCB prototyping machines are categorized into two main types: desktop and integrated. Desktop type machines are compact, user-friendly, and suitable for small-scale prototyping, often used by hobbyists and small businesses for quick, cost-effective circuit designs. Integrated type machines, on the other hand, combine multiple functions such as printing, milling, and soldering, catering to larger companies or research facilities that require high precision and efficiency in batch production. Both types address different needs in the PCB development process.</p></p>
