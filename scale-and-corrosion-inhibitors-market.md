<p><p>Scale and corrosion inhibitors are chemical compounds used to prevent the formation of scale deposits and corrosion in industrial systems, such as pipelines, boilers, cooling towers, and oilfield equipment. Scale inhibitors work by disrupting the crystallization process of minerals like calcium carbonate and calcium sulfate, while corrosion inhibitors form a protective layer on metal surfaces to prevent oxidation and degradation. These inhibitors are essential in industries like oil and gas, power generation, water treatment, and manufacturing to enhance operational efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and extend equipment lifespan.</p><p>The Scale and Corrosion Inhibitors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period. The Asia-Pacific region is anticipated to witness significant growth due to rapid industrialization and infrastructure development.
