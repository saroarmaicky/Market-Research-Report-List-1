<p><p>The milk lactometer market features two primary types: ordinary lactometers and those equipped with thermometers. Ordinary lactometers measure the specific gravity of milk to assess its quality, primarily indicating water content. Meanwhile, lactometers with thermometers also provide temperature readings, enhancing accuracy by accounting for temperature variations affecting density. This added functionality helps ensure more precise quality control in dairy processing. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining milk standards and ensuring safe, high-quality dairy products for consumers.</p></p>
