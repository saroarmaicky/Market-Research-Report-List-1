<p><p>Cloud management solutions are categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Public Cloud**: Services offered over the internet by third-party providers, allowing scalable resources at lower costs for businesses. </p><p>2. **Private Cloud**: Exclusive environments for individual organizations, providing enhanced security and control over data and infrastructure. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud**: A combination of public and private clouds, enabling data and application sharing between them for flexibility and optimized resources. </p><p>4. **Multi-Cloud**: The use of multiple cloud services from different providers, optimizing performance and avoiding vendor lock-in while enhancing redundancy and resilience.</p></p>
