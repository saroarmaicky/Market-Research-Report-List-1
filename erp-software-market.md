<p><ul><li>On Premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-Based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ERP software market is primarily divided into two main types: On-Premise ERP and Cloud-Based ERP. On-Premise ERP solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but often requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based ERP operates on remote servers, providing scalability, easier access from anywhere, and lower initial investments. Companies choose between these options based on their specific needs for flexibility, budget, and IT infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
