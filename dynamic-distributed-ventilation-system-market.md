<p><p>Dynamic Distributed Ventilation Systems (DDVS) encompass two primary market types: full ventilation and local ventilation. Full ventilation systems ensure a comprehensive airflow throughout an entire space, promoting uniform air quality and temperature control. In contrast, local ventilation focuses on targeted areas to quickly remove pollutants or excess heat, enhancing efficiency and comfort. Both types are integral to optimizing indoor environments, improving energy efficiency, and responding to specific ventilation needs based on usage patterns and building design.</p></p>
