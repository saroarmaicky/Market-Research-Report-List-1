<p><p>The heart transplant market encompasses several key types: Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs), Total Artificial Hearts (TAHs), and Others. VADs are mechanical pumps used to support heart function or blood flow in individuals with weakened hearts, often as a bridge to transplant. TAHs replace the heart entirely for patients with end-stage heart failure who are not transplant candidates. The "Others" category includes various supportive technologies and emerging therapies that enhance cardiac function or facilitate recovery, broadening treatment possibilities in advanced heart disease.</p></p>
