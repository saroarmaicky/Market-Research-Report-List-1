<p><p>The cholesterol-lowering drugs market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Statins** - Widely prescribed, these drugs reduce cholesterol production in the liver, effectively lowering LDL levels. </p><p>2. **Cholesterol Absorption Inhibitors** - These medications limit the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the intestines. </p><p>3. **Fibrates** - Primarily used to lower triglycerides, fibrates also modestly impact cholesterol levels. </p><p>4. **PCSK9 Inhibitors** - A newer class of injectable drugs, they enhance the liver's ability to remove LDL from the bloodstream, leading to significant reductions in cholesterol levels.</p></p>
