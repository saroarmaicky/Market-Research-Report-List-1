<p><p>Heat and Moisture Exchangers (HMEs) are medical devices used to conserve heat and moisture during mechanical ventilation. The market comprises two main types: straight style and elbow style HMEs. Straight style HMEs offer a straightforward, linear design, ideal for direct ventilation pathways, while elbow style HMEs feature a bent design, facilitating better placement in restricted spaces. Both types improve patient comfort and reduce complications related to dehydration and hypothermia during respiratory support, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
