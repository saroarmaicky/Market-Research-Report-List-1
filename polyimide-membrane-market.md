<p><strong>The Polyimide Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Both Benzenoid</li><li>Biphenyl Polyimide Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide membrane market is primarily divided into two types: benzenoid and biphenyl polyimide membranes. Benzenoid polyimide membranes are derived from traditional aromatic structures, offering excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various applications in electronics and aerospace. In contrast, biphenyl polyimide membranes feature biphenyl units that enhance flexibility and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including filtration, insulation, and packaging, due to their unique properties.</p></p>
