<p><strong>The Petroleum Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>Modified Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum resin market comprises several types, including C5, C9, C5/C9, and modified petroleum resin. C5 petroleum resin is derived from cyclopentadiene and is primarily used in adhesives and coatings. C9 petroleum resin, obtained from styrene derivatives, is valued for its compatibility in inks and sealants. C5/C9 petroleum resin combines properties of both C5 and C9 resins, enhancing versatility. Modified petroleum resins are chemically altered for improved performance, catering to specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
