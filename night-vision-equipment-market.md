<p><strong>The Night Vision Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night vision equipment market consists of two main types: image intensifier devices and thermal imaging systems. Image intensifiers amplify existing light, allowing users to see in low-light conditions by enhancing visible light. In contrast, thermal imaging captures the infrared radiation emitted by objects, enabling visibility in complete darkness by detecting heat signatures. Both technologies serve various applications, including military, law enforcement, surveillance, and outdoor activities, enhancing situational awareness and safety in challenging environments.</p></p>
