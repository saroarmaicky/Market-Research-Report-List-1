<p><strong>The Flexible AC Transmission System (FACTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) enhance the controllability and power transfer capability of AC transmission systems. The market for FACTS includes two main types: shunt compensation and series compensation. Shunt compensation involves the use of reactive power support devices, like static VAR compensators, to improve voltage stability and reduce losses. In contrast, series compensation uses devices such as Thyristor-controlled series compensators to increase power flow and stabilize voltage levels across transmission lines, thereby optimizing overall grid performance.</p></p>
