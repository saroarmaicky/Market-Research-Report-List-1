<p><p>Oxidation Reduction Potential (ORP) electrodes are essential for measuring the redox potential in various applications, such as water treatment and environmental monitoring. The market for ORP electrodes is primarily divided into two types: single junction and double junction. Single junction electrodes feature a single electrolyte pathway, making them simpler and more cost-effective but susceptible to contamination. In contrast, double junction electrodes offer enhanced performance and stability with a second junction that provides additional protection against interference and extends the electrode's lifespan.</p></p>
