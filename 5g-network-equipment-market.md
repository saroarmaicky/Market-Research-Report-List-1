<p><p>The 5G network equipment market encompasses various types of devices essential for building and enhancing 5G networks. Macro cells provide wide coverage, while small cells enhance capacity in densely populated areas. Remote Radio Units (RRUs) and Active Antenna Units (AAUs) facilitate efficient signal transmission. RF filters ensure signal integrity, and Baseband Units (BBUs) handle data processing. Phase shifters manage beamforming for improved coverage, and energy supply equipment ensures reliability in powering these devices, collectively supporting the robust infrastructure of 5G networks.</p></p>
