<p><strong>The Polyimide Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Spinning Process</li><li>Dry Spinning Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Polyimide Fiber Market** includes types like **Wet Spinning** and **Dry Spinning** processes. In **Wet Spinning**, the polymer solution is extruded into a coagulation bath, where fibers solidify through solvent exchange, producing high-strength, heat-resistant fibers ideal for harsh environments. **Dry Spinning** involves extruding the polymer solution into a heated chamber, evaporating the solvent to form fibers, resulting in smoother, finer fibers suitable for lightweight applications. Both methods cater to industries like aerospace, electronics, and automotive, leveraging polyimide's exceptional thermal and chemical resistance.</p></p>
