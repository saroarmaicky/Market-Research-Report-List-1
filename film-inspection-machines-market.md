<p><p>Film inspection machines are categorized into two main types: fixed and portable. Portable machines, on the other hand, are designed for mobility, allowing users to conduct inspections at various locations or during transport. These are ideal for on-site checks or smaller operations needing flexibility without compromising inspection quality.</p></p>
