<p><p>The medical imaging diagnostic equipment market comprises various imaging modalities, including Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Computed Tomography (CT), Ultrasonography (USG), and Nuclear Medicine Imaging (NMI). MRI utilizes strong magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed internal images, particularly of soft tissues. CT combines X-ray images for cross-sectional views, enhancing accuracy in diagnosis. USG employs sound waves to produce real-time images, widely used in obstetrics. NMI uses radioactive substances for functional imaging, crucial in evaluating diseases like cancer.</p></p>
