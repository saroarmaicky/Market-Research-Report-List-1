<p><p>The decorative plastic paper laminates market includes three main types: low pressure laminates, high pressure laminates, and edge banding. Low pressure laminates are created by bonding a decorative paper sheet with a resin under moderate heat and pressure, ideal for affordable applications. High pressure laminates involve intense heat and pressure for enhanced durability and resistance, making them suitable for commercial use. Edge banding, on the other hand, involves applying a plastic laminate to the exposed edges of materials, providing a finished look and added protection.</p></p>
