<p><p>The IC socket market comprises various types catering to different applications. Dual-in-line Memory Module (DIMM) sockets are designed for memory modules, facilitating easy installation and replacement. Production sockets are used for assembling ICs on printed circuit boards, ensuring efficient manufacturing. Test and burn-in sockets serve for testing IC performance under extreme conditions, ensuring reliability. Dual-in-line Package (DIP) sockets accommodate traditional chips, while specialty sockets are customized for unique designs or requirements, offering versatility across electronic industries.</p></p>
