<p><p>The adhesive papers market is categorized primarily into flexographic printing and letterpress printing. Flexographic printing utilizes flexible relief plates for high-speed printing on various substrates, making it ideal for large-volume production of labels and packaging. In contrast, letterpress printing involves raised surfaces for transferring ink onto paper, providing a tactile quality that appeals to specialty and custom applications. Both methods cater to diverse industries, ensuring high-quality results while meeting specific design and functionality requirements for adhesive paper products.</p></p>
