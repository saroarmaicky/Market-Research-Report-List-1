<p><p>The BioMedical Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) market comprises two primary types: one-component and two-component liquid rubber. One-component LSR is pre-filled with curing agents, simplifying the application process and reducing handling time, making it ideal for medical devices requiring rapid production. In contrast, two-component LSR consists of a base and a curing agent that must be mixed before use, offering greater flexibility in formulation and properties, suitable for complex medical applications. Both types provide biocompatibility and durability essential for biomedical applications.</p></p>
