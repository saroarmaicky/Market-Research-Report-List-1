<p><p>The epoxy coated prestressed concrete strand market is categorized into three main types: 2-wire strand, 3-wire strand, and 7-wire strand. </p><p>The 2-wire strand offers lower prestressing force and is typically used for lighter applications. The 3-wire strand provides a balance between strength and flexibility, ideal for moderate load requirements. The 7-wire strand is the most robust, delivering high strength and is commonly utilized in heavy-duty construction projects, offering excellent durability and corrosion resistance, especially in harsh environments.</p></p>
