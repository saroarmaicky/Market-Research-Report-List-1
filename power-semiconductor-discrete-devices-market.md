<p><p>The power semiconductor discrete devices market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Diodes** are used for rectification in power applications.</p><p>2. **IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors)** combine the benefits of MOSFETs and BJTs, ideal for high voltage and switching applications.</p><p>3. **MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors)** are preferred for their efficiency in low-voltage and fast-switching tasks.</p><p>4. **BJTs (Bipolar Junction Transistors)** provide high current capacity and are utilized in amplification.</p><p>5. **Thyristors** are crucial for controlling high power in AC applications, providing reliable switching capabilities.</p></p>
