<p><p>Desulfurization and denitrification are essential processes aimed at reducing harmful emissions from industrial activities and power generation. Desulfurization removes sulfur oxides (SOx) from exhaust gases, which are mainly produced from the combustion of fossil fuels. This process enhances air quality and helps industries comply with stringent environmental regulations. Denitrification focuses on reducing nitrogen oxides (NOx), which contribute to air pollution and respiratory issues. Recent trends indicate a growing adoption of advanced technologies, such as selective catalytic reduction (SCR) and flue gas desulfurization (FGD), aimed at enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs.
