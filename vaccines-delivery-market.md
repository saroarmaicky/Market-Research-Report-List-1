<p><p>Vaccines delivery market encompasses various types, including biological vectors, chemical carriers, and others. Biological vectors utilize living organisms, like viruses or bacteria, to deliver vaccine components directly into cells, enhancing immune responses. Chemical carriers involve synthetic or naturally derived substances that encapsulate vaccines, facilitating targeted delivery and improved stability. Other delivery methods may include microneedles, transdermal patches, or aerosol forms. Each type aims to maximize vaccine efficacy, reduce side effects, and improve patient compliance in immunization programs.</p></p>
