Impact riveting robots utilize high-speed, short strokes to join materials, ideal for high-volume production with minimal setup. Orbital riveting robots apply a rotational force to create strong, reliable joints, suitable for precision applications in industries like aerospace and automotive. Other types may include specialized or hybrid robots designed for unique riveting challenges, expanding the automation landscape in manufacturing and enhancing efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
