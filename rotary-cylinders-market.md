<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Rotary Cylinder</li><li>Hydraulic Rotary Cylinder</li><li>Electric Rotary Cylinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary cylinders market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric rotary cylinders. Pneumatic rotary cylinders utilize compressed air to generate motion, making them ideal for applications requiring quick, lightweight operations. Hydraulic rotary cylinders use pressurized fluid, providing high power and torque, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Electric rotary cylinders, powered by electric motors, offer precise control and efficiency, making them popular in automated systems. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on power, speed, and precision requirements.</p></p>
