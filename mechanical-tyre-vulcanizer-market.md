<p><ul><li>AFV</li><li>BOM</li><li>AVBO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Tyre Vulcanizer market consists of three main types: Automatic Feed Vulcanization (AFV), Batch Operated Machines (BOM), and Automated Vulcanization Batch Operations (AVBO). AFV systems enhance efficiency by automating the feeding process, suited for high-volume production. BOM, on the other hand, allows for flexible batch processing, ideal for varied production needs. AVBO integrates advanced automation to optimize batch control, ensuring consistent quality and reduced labor costs. Together, these technologies cater to diverse manufacturing requirements in the tyre industry.</p></p>
