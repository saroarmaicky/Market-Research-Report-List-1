Tonometers measure intraocular pressure. Operation microscopes are essential for surgical procedures. Autorefractors and keratometers assess vision and corneal curvature, respectively. Ophthalmoscopes and retinoscopes are used for examining the fundus and assessing refractive errors. Other equipment complements these primary tools.</p></p>
