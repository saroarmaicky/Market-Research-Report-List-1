<p><strong>The Building Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Rough</li><li>Double Rough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building geomembrane market is categorized into single rough and double rough types, based on surface texture. Single rough geomembranes feature a textured surface on one side, enhancing friction and stability, making them suitable for applications like landfill liners and pond liners. In contrast, double rough geomembranes have textured surfaces on both sides, providing increased friction, enhancing drainage, and reducing sliding risks. This makes them ideal for more demanding applications like slope liners and containment barriers.</p></p>
