<p><strong>The EUV Resist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Resist</li><li>Negative Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EUV resist market is categorized into positive and negative resists, essential for extreme ultraviolet lithography. Positive resists become soluble when exposed to EUV light, allowing for detailed pattern transfer. Conversely, negative resists harden upon exposure, creating a durable pattern through the cross-linking of materials. Each type caters to specific applications in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing resolution, sensitivity, and process compatibility. The choice between positive and negative resist depends on the desired outcomes in chip design and production efficiency.</p></p>
