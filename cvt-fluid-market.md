<p><p>CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission) fluid is a specialized lubricant designed for vehicles equipped with CVT systems. It ensures smooth operation, reduces friction, and maintains optimal performance by providing thermal stability, wear protection, and efficient power transfer. Unlike traditional transmission fluids, CVT fluid is formulated to meet the unique demands of CVT systems, which rely on a belt-and-pulley mechanism for seamless gear transitions.</p><p>The CVT Fluid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing adoption of CVT systems in passenger vehicles due to their fuel efficiency and smoother driving experience. Rising demand for automatic transmissions, particularly in emerging economies, is also contributing to market expansion. Additionally, advancements in automotive technology and the development of high-performance CVT fluids are fueling market growth.</p><p>Key trends in the CVT Fluid Market include the shift toward synthetic and semi-synthetic fluids, which offer superior performance and longevity. Manufacturers are focusing on eco-friendly formulations to meet stringent environmental regulations. The growing popularity of electric and hybrid vehicles is also influencing the market, as these vehicles often incorporate CVT systems. Overall, the market is poised for steady growth, supported by technological innovations and increasing vehicle production worldwide.</p></p>

Mineral oil, derived from crude oil, is more cost-effective but provides limited performance under extreme conditions.
