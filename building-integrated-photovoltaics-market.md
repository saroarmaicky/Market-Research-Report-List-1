<p><p>The Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) market features three main types: Single Crystal Silicon, Polycrystalline Silicon, and Thin Film. Single Crystal Silicon offers high efficiency and longevity, making it ideal for urban environments. Polycrystalline Silicon is cost-effective and widely used, striking a balance between performance and price. Thin Film technology is lightweight and flexible, suitable for unconventional surfaces, but typically has lower efficiency. Each type contributes uniquely to integrating solar energy solutions into building designs, enhancing sustainability.</p></p>
