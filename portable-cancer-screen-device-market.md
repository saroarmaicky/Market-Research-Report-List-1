<p><p>The portable cancer screening device market encompasses various technologies, including Infrared Thermography and Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy devices. Infrared Thermography detects abnormal temperature patterns indicative of cancerous tissues by using thermal imaging. In contrast, Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy measures tissue resistance to electrical currents, identifying cellular irregularities associated with tumors. Both technologies offer non-invasive, rapid screening options, enhancing early detection capabilities and improving patient outcomes, while also being suitable for use in diverse settings outside traditional clinical environments.</p></p>
