<p><ul><li>Batch</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic concrete mixer market is primarily categorized into two types: batch mixers and continuous mixers. Batch mixers produce concrete by combining ingredients in fixed amounts, allowing for high-quality outputs and customization for specific projects. In contrast, continuous mixers continuously feed materials and produce concrete without interruption, making them ideal for large-scale operations where consistent, high-volume production is essential. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying demands in construction and infrastructure development.</p></p>
