<p><p>The Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools market is divided into two key segments: Bulk Deformation (BD) and Sheet Metal Working (SMW). Bulk Deformation focuses on shaping metals through processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling, manipulating larger volumes and thicker materials. In contrast, Sheet Metal Working encompasses methods such as stamping, bending, and cutting, targeting thinner metal sheets for various applications. Both segments are essential in manufacturing industries, facilitating the production of components for automotive, aerospace, and construction sectors.</p></p>
