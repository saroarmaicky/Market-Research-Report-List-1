<p><p>The 3D scanners market encompasses various types, notably laser scanners and portable CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) based scanners. Laser scanners capture detailed geometric data of objects using laser beams, allowing for high-accuracy 3D models, ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, portable CMM-based scanners utilize touch probes and optical systems for precise measurements of smaller objects in varied environments, offering flexibility and convenience. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, engineering, and heritage preservation, enhancing quality control and product development.</p></p>
