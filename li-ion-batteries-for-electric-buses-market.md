<p><strong>The Li-ion Batteries for Electric Buses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFP</li><li>NMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Li-ion batteries used in electric buses primarily include LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) and NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) types. LFP batteries are known for their thermal stability, safety, and longevity, making them suitable for urban transport. In contrast, NMC batteries offer higher energy density, enabling longer range and better performance, particularly for intercity travel. The choice between LFP and NMC largely depends on the specific requirements of the bus operations, including range, cost, and operational safety considerations.</p></p>
