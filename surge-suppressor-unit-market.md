<p><p>The surge suppressor unit market can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Switch Type**: This uses electrical switches to interrupt excessive surges, protecting equipment by diverting current.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Pressure Limiting Type**: This type regulates pressure levels, preventing damage from sudden pressure spikes in hydraulic systems.</p><p>3. **Flow or Choke Type**: This design restricts fluid flow to absorb energy surges, minimizing impact on pipelines and associated machinery. </p><p>Each type serves to enhance system reliability and safety in various applications.</p></p>
