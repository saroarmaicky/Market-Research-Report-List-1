<p><p>The process thermostats market can be divided into two main types: line-voltage thermostats and low-voltage thermostats. Line-voltage thermostats directly control heating or cooling devices that operate on high voltage, typically found in residential applications. In contrast, low-voltage thermostats manage systems that operate on lower voltage, such as central heating and cooling systems in commercial settings. Each type addresses specific energy management needs and regulatory requirements, influencing their application across various sectors.</p></p>
