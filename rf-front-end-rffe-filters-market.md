<p><ul><li>SAW Filters</li><li>BAW Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF Front-end (RFFE) filters market primarily consists of Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters utilize surface acoustic waves to process high-frequency signals, offering advantages like compact size and low cost, making them ideal for consumer electronics. In contrast, BAW filters utilize bulk acoustic waves, providing superior performance in high-frequency applications and higher power handling, making them suitable for advanced telecommunications and 5G networks. Both types cater to diverse communication applications.</p></p>
