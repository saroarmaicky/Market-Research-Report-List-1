<p><strong>The Reflective Traffic Signage Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microprismatic</li><li>Glass Beads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective traffic signage films enhance visibility and safety by incorporating two primary types: microprismatic and glass beads. Microprismatic films utilize a series of tiny, prisms that reflect light efficiently, offering superior brightness and durability, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, glass beads films incorporate tiny glass spheres that reflect light, providing excellent visibility but generally less brightness compared to microprismatic options. Both types are critical for effective traffic signage, supporting road safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
