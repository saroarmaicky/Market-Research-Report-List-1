<p><p>Feed acidifiers are organic acids used in animal nutrition to enhance feed preservation, improve gut health, and boost overall animal performance. Propionic acid helps inhibit mold growth, while formic acid aids in digestion and reduces pathogens. Lactic acid promotes beneficial bacteria in the gut, whereas citric acid enhances mineral absorption and acts as a preservative. Malic acid improves energy metabolism, and sorbic acid provides antimicrobial properties. Together, these acidifiers contribute to better feed efficiency and animal welfare in various livestock sectors.</p></p>
