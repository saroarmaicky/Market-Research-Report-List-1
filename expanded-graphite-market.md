<p><p>The expanded graphite market is categorized into three main forms: packing form, gasket form, and weave form. Packing form involves compressed expanded graphite used for sealing applications, providing excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Gasket form consists of sheets or cut shapes used to create seals between surfaces, ensuring leak prevention in various industries. Weave form refers to graphite fibers woven into a fabric, suitable for high-temperature applications and offering flexibility while maintaining durability and resistance to wear and tear.</p></p>
