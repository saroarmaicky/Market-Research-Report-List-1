<p><p>The immunotherapy drugs market consists of several key types. Monoclonal antibodies are engineered to target specific antigens, enhancing immune response against cancers and diseases. Adult vaccines stimulate the immune system to prevent infections. Checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that suppress immune responses, allowing T-cells to attack tumors effectively. Interferons alpha and beta boost the immune system against viral infections and some cancers. Interleukins act as signaling molecules to enhance immune cell activity, while the "others" category encompasses emerging therapies and novel immunomodulators.</p></p>
