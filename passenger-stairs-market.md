<p><p>The passenger stairs market is categorized into two primary types: self-propelled and towable stairs. Self-propelled passenger stairs are equipped with their own power source, enabling them to move independently and access various aircraft without external assistance. Towable passenger stairs, on the other hand, require a towing vehicle for mobility, offering flexibility in various ground handling operations. Both types enhance airport efficiency by facilitating passenger boarding and disembarkation from aircraft of different sizes and designs.</p></p>
