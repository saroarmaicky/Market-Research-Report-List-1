<p><p>The plastic cable cleat market consists of three primary types: single type, multicore type, and trefoil type. Single type cleats are designed for securing individual cables, ensuring stability and protection. Multicore type cleats accommodate multiple cables bundled together, providing effective organization and support. Trefoil type cleats are specifically engineered for three-phase electrical cables, maintaining optimal spacing and minimizing electromagnetic interference. Each type serves distinct applications, enhancing safety and efficiency in electrical installations across various industries.</p></p>
