<p><p>The freeze-drying masks market encompasses two primary types: dry powder masks and lyophilized stock solution masks. Dry powder masks consist of dehydrated ingredients that rehydrate upon contact with water, offering convenience and long shelf life. In contrast, lyophilized stock solution masks contain a pre-formulated blend of active ingredients that are freeze-dried and require mixing before application. Both types leverage freeze-drying technology to preserve the potency of skincare elements, enhancing user experience with effective, easy-to-store products.</p></p>
