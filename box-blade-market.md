<p><strong>The Box Blade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-tine</li><li>Rear-tine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The box blade market is divided into two main types: front-tine and rear-tine. Front-tine box blades have tines positioned in the front, ideal for breaking up hard soil and providing precise control, often favored for landscaping and smaller projects. Rear-tine box blades feature tines at the back, offering better soil mixing and depth control, making them suitable for larger areas and heavy-duty tasks. Both types cater to different user needs in earthmoving and landscaping applications.</p></p>
