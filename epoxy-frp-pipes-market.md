<p><p>The Epoxy FRP (Fiber Reinforced Polymer) pipe market encompasses various manufacturing methods, notably Hot Rolled Steel Pipe and Cold Drawn Pipe. Hot Rolled Steel Pipes are produced by rolling steel at high temperatures, offering enhanced ductility and strength, suitable for large-diameter applications. In contrast, Cold Drawn Pipes involve drawing metal at room temperature, resulting in a smoother finish, higher dimensional accuracy, and improved mechanical properties. Both types are utilized in various industries, including construction and oil and gas, due to their durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
